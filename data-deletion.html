Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your personal data from Umrati, please follow the steps below:

Option 1: Remove App from Facebook

  1. Go to Facebook Settings
  2. Click "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find Umrati and click "Remove"
  4. This will revoke all data access granted to our app

Option 2: Email Request

You can also request manual data deletion by emailing us:

  • Email: Teamumrati@gmail.com
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request
  • Include: Your Facebook User ID or the email associated with your Facebook account

We will process your request within 7 days and confirm the deletion.
